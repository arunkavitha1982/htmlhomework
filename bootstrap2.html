WELCOME TO SHREE BHARATH VIDYAASHRAM

Affiliated to central board of secondary Education, New Delhi, Premium CBSE School, CBSE Affiliation No:1931246.
“SRI LAKSHMI AMMAL EDUCATIONAL TRUST” With a view to extend its services from Medicine to Education started another project by establishing co-educational CBSE Schools of International Standard in the academic year 2016-2017. Dr.S.Jagathrakshakan, former Minister of the State, Govt. Of India And Chairman of Bharath Group Of Institutions founded this school with a vision to provide education to the local community with Global Standard. Dr.J.Sundeep Aanand, President of Bharath Foundation and Bharath Group of Educational Institutions is always the back bone for our school’s development. “Shree Bharath Vidyaashram” – The first CBSE School is situated on the Agaram Main Road, Tambaram, Chennai. The school caters to the holistic development of Children at World level. The school had a humble beginning with 200 students from classes Pre-KG to Class 9th and in the very subsequent year found an overwhelming response from the parents with the student strength in the academic year nearing 1000.
